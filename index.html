📖 Companion Rules
🔮 FAMILIAR (Find Familiar spell)
Familiar tidak bisa attack. Tapi bisa Help action (Adv saat kamu adjacent). Kamu bisa lihat/dengar melalui indra familiar (Bonus Action, max 10 menit/day). Jika mati: hilang sampai cast lagi (10gp components). Bisa dismiss ke pocket dimension.
🐺 RANGER BEAST COMPANION
Proficiency = Ranger's proficiency bonus. HP = 5× ranger level. Bisa attack, kena buff. Aksi Beast: Attack, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Help. Kamu harus gunakan Bonus Action untuk perintah. Jika mati: summon lagi saat Long Rest.
✨ SUMMONS (Summon spells)
HP = nilai di spell + modifier. Attack bonus = Spell Attack bonus. Bisa diperintah Bonus Action. Hilang saat HP 0 atau concentration putus.
⚠ ATURAN UMUM
Companion punya initiative sendiri. Mereka roll terpisah di combat. Kamu mengontrol aksi mereka di giliran mereka (kecuali ada aturan berbeda). Mereka bisa jadi target serangan musuh.

STR — STRENGTH
Kekuatan fisik. Dipakai untuk melee attacks, Athletics, bawa beban, paksa pintu. Saving throw STR: resist efek fisik paksa (Thunderwave, dll).
DEX — DEXTERITY
Kelincahan & refleks. Dipakai untuk ranged attacks, finesse weapons, Initiative, AC (light/medium armor), Acrobatics, Sleight of Hand, Stealth. Saving throw DEX: hindari efek area (Fireball, dll).
CON — CONSTITUTION
Daya tahan & kesehatan. Menentukan HP (ditambahkan tiap level). Dipakai untuk Concentration saves, menahan racun/penyakit. Saving throw CON: tahan efek yang melemahkan tubuh.
INT — INTELLIGENCE
Kecerdasan & memori. Dipakai untuk Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature, Religion. Spellcasting ability untuk Wizard. Saving throw INT: resist ilusi & mind control tertentu.
WIS — WISDOM
Intuisi & persepsi. Dipakai untuk Passive Perception, Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Perception, Survival. Spellcasting ability untuk Cleric, Druid, Ranger. Saving throw WIS: resist charm & fear.
CHA — CHARISMA
Kepribadian & pengaruh. Dipakai untuk Deception, Intimidation, Performance, Persuasion. Spellcasting ability untuk Bard, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock. Saving throw CHA: resist efek yang mengubah identitas/keberadaan.

⚔ Combat Stats Explained
ARMOR CLASS (AC)
Angka yang harus disamai/dilebihi attacker untuk mengenaimu. Base AC tergantung armor:
• No armor: 10 + DEX mod
• Light: base + DEX mod
• Medium: base + DEX mod (maks +2)
• Heavy: flat number, no DEX
• Shield: +2 AC
HIT POINTS (HP)
Nyawa karaktermu. Saat HP = 0, kamu Unconscious dan mulai Death Saves. HP max = Hit Die max + CON mod di level 1, lalu Hit Die avg + CON mod tiap level berikutnya.
INITIATIVE
Menentukan urutan giliran dalam combat. Roll = d20 + DEX mod. Lebih tinggi = lebih dahulu bertindak.
ATTACK ROLL
d20 + Attack Bonus vs AC target.
• Melee: d20 + STR mod + Prof (jika proficient)
• Ranged: d20 + DEX mod + Prof
• Finesse: pilih STR atau DEX
• Nat 20 = Critical Hit (double damage dice!)
• Nat 1 = Miss otomatis
SPELL SAVE DC & SPELL ATTACK
Spell Save DC = 8 + Prof Bonus + Spellcasting Mod
Spell Attack Bonus = Prof Bonus + Spellcasting Mod
Spellcasting Mod = INT (Wizard), WIS (Cleric/Druid), CHA (Bard/Sorc/Warlock/Paladin)
SAVING THROWS
d20 + Ability Mod [+ Prof Bonus] vs DC efek.
Tiap class punya 2 saving throw proficiency. Cek class feature kamu.
⏱ Action Economy
🎯 ACTION (1 per turn)
Attack, Cast a Spell, Dash, Dodge, Disengage, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Use Object, Grapple, Shove.
⚡ BONUS ACTION (0–1 per turn)
Hanya tersedia jika ada class feature/spell yang memberikannya. Contoh: Two-Weapon Fighting, Cunning Action (Rogue), Misty Step (spell), Bardic Inspiration.
🔄 REACTION (0–1 per round)
Di luar giliranmu. Contoh: Opportunity Attack, Shield spell, Counterspell, Parry, Sentinel feat. Reset di awal giliranmu.
🚶 MOVEMENT (Speed per turn)
Bisa dipecah sebelum/sesudah action. Bisa dikurangi efek kondisi. Prone = ½ speed untuk berdiri. Difficult terrain = 2ft per 1ft.
💬 FREE INTERACTION
1 free object interaction per turn (draw weapon, open door, pick up item). Lebih dari 1 = butuh Action.
🎲 Advantage & Disadvantage
ADVANTAGE
Roll 2d20, ambil yang lebih tinggi. Contoh: Allied Help action, Prone target (melee), Invisible attacker, Reckless Attack.
DISADVANTAGE
Roll 2d20, ambil yang lebih rendah. Contoh: Prone target (ranged), Invisible target, Poisoned, Restrained, Exhausted.
Catatan penting: Jika ada keduanya (1 adv + 1 disadv), hasilnya normal — tidak peduli berapa banyak sumber masing-masing. Multiple advantage/disadvantage tidak stack.

⚔ BARBARIAN
HD: d12Armor: Light, Medium, ShieldsSaves: STR, CON
Rage: Bonus Action. Advantage STR checks/saves, +2/3/4 damage (scales), resistance to bludg/pierce/slash. 2–6 uses/Long Rest.
Unarmored Defense: AC = 10 + DEX mod + CON mod (no armor).
Reckless Attack: Advantage on STR attacks, but enemies get Adv vs you.
Extra Attack (Lv 5): 2 attacks per Action.
Brutal Critical (Lv 9): +1 extra damage die on crit.
Subclasses: Berserker, Totem Warrior, Zealot, Storm Herald, Wild Magic, Beast, Ancestral Guardian
🎵 BARD
HD: d8Armor: LightSaves: DEX, CHASpells: CHA
Bardic Inspiration: Bonus Action, give ally a d6–d12 die to add to roll. CHA mod uses/Long Rest (or Short Rest at Lv 5).
Jack of All Trades (Lv 2): Add half prof bonus to any check you're not proficient in.
Song of Rest (Lv 2): Allies regain extra HP when spending hit dice on Short Rest.
Expertise (Lv 3, 10): Double prof bonus on 2 chosen skills.
Magical Secrets (Lv 10): Learn 2 spells from any class list.
Subclasses: Lore, Valor, Glamour, Swords, Whispers, Creation, Eloquence
✝ CLERIC
HD: d8Armor: Light, Medium, ShieldsSaves: WIS, CHASpells: WIS
Spellcasting: Prepare WIS mod + level spells. Domain spells always prepared.
Channel Divinity (Lv 2): Turn Undead or domain-specific effect. 1–3 uses/Short Rest.
Destroy Undead (Lv 5): Turn Undead destroys low CR undead outright.
Divine Intervention (Lv 10): Roll d100 ≤ Cleric level = deity intervenes.
Domains: Life, Light, War, Knowledge, Nature, Trickery, Tempest, Death, Forge, Grave, Order, Peace, Twilight
🌿 DRUID
HD: d8Armor: Light, Medium, Shields (non-metal)Saves: INT, WISSpells: WIS
Wild Shape (Lv 2): Transform into beasts (CR limit scales). 2 uses/Short Rest.
Spellcasting: Prepare WIS mod + level spells. Access to full Druid spell list.
Timeless Body (Lv 18): Age 1/10 rate, immune to magical aging.
Beast Spells (Lv 18): Cast spells while in Wild Shape form.
Circles: Land, Moon, Spores, Stars, Wildfire, Dreams, Shepherd, Twilight
⚔ FIGHTER
HD: d10Armor: All + ShieldsSaves: STR, CON
Fighting Style (Lv 1): Archery (+2 ranged), Defense (+1 AC), Dueling (+2 dmg 1H), GWF, Protection, Two-Weapon.
Second Wind (Lv 1): Bonus Action: heal 1d10 + level. 1×/Short Rest.
Action Surge (Lv 2): Extra full Action. 1–2×/Short Rest.
Extra Attack (Lv 5/11/20): 2/3/4 attacks per Action.
Indomitable (Lv 9): Reroll a failed saving throw. 1–3×/Long Rest.
Archetypes: Champion, Battle Master, Eldritch Knight, Psi Warrior, Rune Knight, Samurai, Cavalier, Arcane Archer
👊 MONK
HD: d8Armor: NoneSaves: STR, DEX
Martial Arts: Unarmed/monk weapon = DEX or STR, use Martial Arts die (d4→d10). Bonus Action unarmed strike after attack.
Unarmored Defense: AC = 10 + DEX mod + WIS mod.
Ki Points (Lv 2): = Monk level. Recharge Short Rest. Powers: Flurry, Patient Defense, Step of Wind.
Stunning Strike (Lv 5): Spend 1 Ki on hit → CON save or Stunned until end of your next turn.
Evasion (Lv 7): DEX save = no damage on success, half on fail.
Traditions: Open Hand, Shadow, Four Elements, Astral Self, Mercy, Drunken Master, Kensei, Sun Soul
🛡 PALADIN
HD: d10Armor: All + ShieldsSaves: WIS, CHASpells: CHA
Divine Sense (Lv 1): Detect celestials/fiends/undead. CHA mod + 1 uses/Long Rest.
Lay on Hands (Lv 1): Pool = 5 × level HP. Heal or cure disease/poison (5 HP).
Divine Smite (Lv 2): Expend spell slot on hit → 2d8 + 1d8/slot level extra radiant (3d8 vs undead/fiend). Crit doubles dice.
Aura of Protection (Lv 6): Allies within 10 ft add CHA mod to saving throws.
Extra Attack (Lv 5): 2 attacks per Action.
Oaths: Devotion, Ancients, Vengeance, Conquest, Redemption, Glory, Watchers, Oathbreaker
🏹 RANGER
HD: d10Armor: Light, Medium, ShieldsSaves: STR, DEXSpells: WIS
Favored Enemy (Lv 1): Advantage on Survival to track, Intelligence to recall info about chosen enemy types.
Natural Explorer (Lv 1): Bonuses in favored terrain: double prof on INT/WIS checks, ignore difficult terrain, etc.
Hunter's Mark (Lv 1): Bonus Action concentration spell: +1d6 dmg vs marked target, Adv on Perception/Survival to find it.
Extra Attack (Lv 5): 2 attacks per Action.
Conclaves: Hunter, Beastmaster, Gloom Stalker, Horizon Walker, Monster Slayer, Fey Wanderer, Swarmkeeper
🗡 ROGUE
HD: d8Armor: LightSaves: DEX, INT
Sneak Attack (Lv 1): 1d6 per 2 levels extra damage when you have Adv or ally adjacent to target (1× per turn).
Thieves' Cant (Lv 1): Secret language of thieves.
Cunning Action (Lv 2): Bonus Action: Dash, Disengage, or Hide every turn.
Uncanny Dodge (Lv 5): Reaction: halve damage from one attack.
Evasion (Lv 7): DEX save = no damage; fail = half.
Reliable Talent (Lv 11): Treat any prof skill roll of 9 or below as a 10.
Archetypes: Thief, Assassin, Arcane Trickster, Inquisitive, Mastermind, Scout, Swashbuckler, Phantom, Soulknife
✨ SORCERER
HD: d6Armor: NoneSaves: CON, CHASpells: CHA
Sorcery Points (Lv 2): = Level. Recharge Long Rest. Convert to/from spell slots (Flexible Casting).
Metamagic (Lv 3): Modify spells. Options: Careful (protect allies), Distant (2× range), Empowered (+reroll damage), Extended (2× duration), Heightened (DisAdv on save), Quickened (cast as Bonus Action), Subtle (no V/S), Twinned (target 2).
Sorcerous Restoration (Lv 20): Regain 4 Sorcery Points on Short Rest.
Origins: Draconic, Wild Magic, Divine Soul, Shadow, Storm, Aberrant Mind, Clockwork Soul, Lunar
😈 WARLOCK
HD: d8Armor: LightSaves: WIS, CHASpells: CHA
Pact Magic: 1–4 spell slots (always max level), all recharge on Short Rest.
Eldritch Blast (Cantrip): 1d10 force damage, range 120 ft. +1 beam at Lv 5/11/17.
Eldritch Invocations (Lv 2): Permanent magical boons. Examples: Agonizing Blast (+CHA to EB), Devil's Sight (see in darkness), Armor of Shadows (Mage Armor at will).
Pact Boon (Lv 3): Blade (summoned weapon), Chain (improved familiar), Tome (extra cantrips + ritual spells).
Patrons: Archfey, Fiend, Great Old One, Celestial, Hexblade, Fathomless, Genie, Undying, Undead
📖 WIZARD
HD: d6Armor: NoneSaves: INT, WISSpells: INT
Spellbook: Start with 6 spells, copy new ones from scrolls/other books. Prepare INT mod + level spells.
Arcane Recovery (Lv 1): Once/Long Rest after Short Rest: regain spell slots up to half level (rounded up).
Spell Mastery (Lv 18): Choose 1st and 2nd level spell to cast for free at base level.
Signature Spells (Lv 20): Two 3rd-level spells always prepared, cast without slot 1×/Short Rest each.
Schools: Abjuration, Conjuration, Divination, Enchantment, Evocation, Illusion, Necromancy, Transmutation, Bladesinging, Chronurgy, Graviturgy, Order of Scribes, War Magic
🪓 BERSERKER
Barbarian · Available at Level 3
Frenzy (Lv 3): Bonus Action attack every turn while raging. After rage: 1 level of Exhaustion.
Mindless Rage (Lv 6): Immune to Charmed/Frightened while raging.
Retaliation (Lv 14): Reaction attack when damaged by adjacent creature.
Playstyle: Maximum damage output. Trade-off is Exhaustion after heavy use.
🪓 TOTEM WARRIOR
Barbarian · Available at Level 3
Spirit Seeker (Lv 3): Cast Beast Sense and Speak with Animals as rituals.
Totem Spirit (Lv 3): Bear = resistance all dmg while raging (best). Eagle = Disengage as Bonus Action. Wolf = allies Adv vs adjacent enemies.
Aspect of the Beast (Lv 6): Passive bestial feature outside combat.
Totemic Attunement (Lv 14): Additional totem power during rage.
Playstyle: Versatile and powerful. Bear Totem is one of the strongest defensive options in the game.
🪓 ZEALOT
Barbarian · Available at Level 3
Divine Fury (Lv 3): First hit per rage deals +1d6 necrotic or radiant extra damage.
Warrior of the Gods (Lv 3): Resurrection spells cost no material components for you.
Fanatical Focus (Lv 6): Reroll failed save while raging (1×/rage).
Zealous Presence (Lv 10): Allies gain Advantage on attacks and saves for 1 turn (1×/Long Rest).
Playstyle: Frontline damage + hard to kill permanently. Great for death-prone campaigns.
🪓 WILD MAGIC BARBARIAN
Barbarian · Available at Level 3
Magic Awareness (Lv 3): Detect spells/magic items within 60 ft as Action.
Wild Surge (Lv 3): On each rage, roll d8 for random magical effect (teleport, healing, damage, etc).
Bolstering Magic (Lv 6): Give ally +1d3 to attack/check, or regain a spell slot (1–3).
Playstyle: Chaotic support + damage hybrid. High variance, very fun in relaxed campaigns.
🎵 COLLEGE OF LORE
Bard · Available at Level 3
Bonus Proficiencies (Lv 3): 3 skills of your choice from any class list.
Cutting Words (Lv 3): Reaction: use Bardic Inspiration die to subtract from enemy attack, check, or damage roll.
Additional Magical Secrets (Lv 6): Learn 2 spells from any class 4 levels earlier than normal.
Peerless Skill (Lv 14): Add Bardic Inspiration die to ability checks.
Playstyle: Support/utility caster. Best skill-monkey in the game. Excellent in roleplay-heavy campaigns.
🎵 COLLEGE OF VALOR
Bard · Available at Level 3
Bonus Proficiencies (Lv 3): Medium armor, shields, martial weapons.
Combat Inspiration (Lv 3): Allies can add Bardic Inspiration die to AC or weapon damage.
Extra Attack (Lv 6): 2 attacks per Action.
Battle Magic (Lv 14): Bonus Action weapon attack after casting bard spell.
Playstyle: Frontline melee support bard. Good for those who want to fight AND inspire.
🎵 COLLEGE OF GLAMOUR
Bard · Available at Level 3
Mantle of Inspiration (Lv 3): Bonus Action: use inspiration die to give multiple allies Temp HP and a free move.
Enthralling Performance (Lv 3): 1-minute performance: charm up to 5 humanoids for 1 hour.
Mantle of Majesty (Lv 6): Bonus Action: cast Command for free each turn, look like a deity (1×/Long Rest).
Playstyle: Fey-themed crowd control + mass support. Excellent in social and urban campaigns.
🎵 COLLEGE OF SWORDS
Bard · Available at Level 3
Blade Flourish (Lv 3): When you attack, move 10 ft and use Bardic Inspiration die for Defensive (AC), Slashing (AoE), or Mobile (knockback) Flourish.
Extra Attack (Lv 6): 2 attacks per Action.
Master's Flourish (Lv 14): Use d6 for Flourish without spending Inspiration die.
Playstyle: Duelist bard. High mobility, single-target burst. Fun but resource-intensive.
✝ LIFE DOMAIN
Cleric · Available at Level 1
Bonus Proficiency: Heavy armor.
Disciple of Life (Lv 1): Healing spells restore 2 + spell level extra HP.
Preserve Life (Lv 2, Channel): Restore HP equal to 5× level among creatures within 30 ft, up to half max HP each.
Blessed Healer (Lv 6): Healing others also heals you 2–9 HP.
Supreme Healing (Lv 17): Use maximum values for healing dice.
Playstyle: The best healer in the game. Essential for parties without a dedicated healer.
✝ LIGHT DOMAIN
Cleric · Available at Level 1
Bonus Cantrip: Light cantrip.
Warding Flare (Lv 1): Reaction: impose Disadvantage on incoming attack (WIS mod uses/Long Rest).
Radiance of the Dawn (Lv 2, Channel): Dispel magical darkness, deal 2d10 + level radiant to undead/hostiles.
Improved Flare (Lv 6): Use Warding Flare when allies are attacked.
Corona of Light (Lv 17): Sunlight aura, enemies DisAdv on saves vs fire/radiant spells.
Playstyle: Offensive/anti-undead blaster. Good damage output for a cleric.
✝ WAR DOMAIN
Cleric · Available at Level 1
Bonus Proficiencies: Heavy armor, martial weapons.
War Priest (Lv 1): Bonus Action weapon attack after casting cleric spell (WIS mod uses/Long Rest).
Guided Strike (Lv 2, Channel): +10 to attack roll (reaction, after you or ally rolls).
War God's Blessing (Lv 6): Give ally +10 to attack roll via Channel.
Playstyle: Melee frontline cleric. Excellent burst attack + support combo. Very popular.
✝ TRICKERY DOMAIN
Cleric · Available at Level 1
Invoke Duplicity (Lv 2, Channel): Illusory duplicate that you can cast through (Adv when adjacent to it).
Cloak of Shadows (Lv 6, Channel): Turn invisible until attack/spell.
Improved Duplicity (Lv 17): Create 4 duplicates instead of 1.
Playstyle: Infiltration and deception. Fun for social campaigns, less combat-effective than War/Light.
🌿 CIRCLE OF THE MOON
Druid · Available at Level 2
Combat Wild Shape (Lv 2): Wild Shape as Bonus Action. Spend spell slot to heal 1d8/slot level while shapeshifted.
Circle Forms (Lv 2): Wild Shape into CR 1 beasts (scales: Lv 6 → CR 2, Lv 9 → CR 3, etc.).
Elemental Wild Shape (Lv 10): Transform into an Elemental (Air, Earth, Fire, Water).
Playstyle: Tank/melee hybrid. Wildshape into powerful forms and fight on the frontline.
🌿 CIRCLE OF LAND
Druid · Available at Level 2
Bonus Cantrip (Lv 2): Learn one extra druid cantrip.
Natural Recovery (Lv 2): After Short Rest: regain spell slots up to half druid level (1×/Long Rest).
Circle Spells (Lv 3): Always-prepared bonus spells based on terrain (Arctic, Coast, Desert, Forest, Grassland, Mountain, Swamp, Underdark).
Land's Stride (Lv 6): Move through nonmagical difficult terrain freely, avoid plant hazards.
Playstyle: Pure spellcaster druid. More spell slots and terrain-specific utility spells.
🌿 CIRCLE OF SPORES
Druid · Available at Level 2
Halo of Spores (Lv 2): Reaction when creature moves near you: 1d4–1d8 necrotic damage (CON save).
Symbiotic Entity (Lv 2): Spend Wild Shape use: 4× level Temp HP, Halo doubles damage, unarmed = 1d6 poison extra.
Fungal Infestation (Lv 6): Animate slain humanoid/beast as zombie (Reaction).
Playstyle: Melee druid hybrid with undead-creation. Unusual but very effective at mid-levels.
🌿 CIRCLE OF STARS
Druid · Available at Level 2
Star Map (Lv 2): Guidance cantrip always prepared; cast Guiding Bolt without slot (WIS mod/Long Rest).
Starry Form (Lv 2): Bonus Action: glow, choose form — Archer (ranged radiant), Chalice (heals on concentration spells), Dragon (CON checks Adv, immune fear).
Cosmic Omen (Lv 6): Roll die at dawn — Weal (add d6 to allies) or Woe (subtract d6 from enemies).
Playstyle: Flexible support/offense druid. Starry Form is powerful at all tiers.
⚔ CHAMPION
Fighter · Available at Level 3
Improved Critical (Lv 3): Crit on 19–20 (Lv 15: 18–20).
Remarkable Athlete (Lv 7): Add half prof to STR/DEX/CON checks you're not proficient in. +10 ft running jump.
Additional Fighting Style (Lv 10): Choose a second Fighting Style.
Survivor (Lv 18): Regain 5 + CON mod HP at start of your turn if below half HP.
Playstyle: Simple, reliable, consistent. Best for beginners. Devastating with crit-fishing builds (GWM/PAM).
⚔ BATTLE MASTER
Fighter · Available at Level 3
Superiority Dice (Lv 3): d8 dice (4→6, scales). Recharge Short Rest. Spent on Maneuvers.
Maneuvers (Lv 3): 3→5 known. Examples: Riposte (counter-attack on miss), Trip Attack (knock prone), Precision (add die to attack), Rally (give ally THP), Menacing (frighten).
Know Your Enemy (Lv 7): Study creature 1 min → learn comparative stat blocks.
Playstyle: Most versatile Fighter. Great at everything — control, damage, support. Top tier subclass.
⚔ ELDRITCH KNIGHT
Fighter · Available at Level 3
Spellcasting (Lv 3): 1/3 caster (INT). Access to Abjuration + Evocation spells (others limited).
Weapon Bond (Lv 3): Bond 2 weapons — can't be disarmed, teleport them to your hand.
War Magic (Lv 7): Bonus Action attack after casting a cantrip.
Arcane Charge (Lv 15): Teleport up to 30 ft when using Action Surge.
Playstyle: Melee + magic hybrid. Shield spell reaction is incredibly strong. Great survivability.
⚔ PSI WARRIOR
Fighter · Available at Level 3
Psionic Power (Lv 3): Psi dice pool (d6→d12, twice prof). Recharge Short/Long Rest. Powers: Protective Field (THP), Psionic Strike (+dmg), Telekinetic Movement (move objects).
Telekinetic Adept (Lv 7): Psi-Powered Leap (fly 2× speed once/turn) or Telekinetic Thrust (push on hit).
Guarded Mind (Lv 10): Resistance to psychic, break mental effects on yourself.
Playstyle: Psionic control fighter. Fun utility options but slightly behind Battle Master in pure effectiveness.

👊 DRUNKEN MASTER

⚙ CARA KERJA MULTICLASS
Leveling: Pilih class mana yang level-up tiap kali naik level. Level total tetap sama (misal Fighter 5 / Wizard 3 = Level 8 character).
HP: Tambah Hit Die baru sesuai class baru. Level 1 multiclass tidak dapat max HD — pakai average.
Prof Bonus: Berdasarkan total character level, bukan per-class.
Proficiency dari class baru: Hanya dapat sebagian (bukan semua proficiency kelas 1). Lihat tabel di bawah.
Features: Dapat class features sesuai level di masing-masing class, kecuali yang sudah dimiliki (Fighting Style, Extra Attack, dll).
Saving Throw Proficiency: Hanya dari class utama (level 1). Class multiclass tidak memberi saves baru.

✨ SPELL SLOTS MULTICLASS
Spell slot total ditentukan dari jumlah level caster yang digabung, bukan masing-masing class sendiri.
Full caster (Wiz/Clr/Drd/Brd/Sorc): hitung semua level.
Half caster (Pal/Rng): hitung ½ level (bulatkan ke bawah).
Third caster (Eldritch Knight/Arcane Trickster): hitung ⅓ level.
Warlock: Pact Magic terpisah. Slot-nya tidak digabung dengan multiclass slots.
Contoh: Fighter 2 / Wizard 3 → Caster Level = 0 (Fighter non-caster) + 3 = Level 3 spell slots. Paladin 4 / Sorcerer 3 → Caster Level = 2 + 3 = 5 → gunakan kolom Level 5 pada tabel Full Caster.
Spell yang diketahui/disiapkan tetap terpisah per class. Slot bisa digunakan untuk spell dari class manapun.
🔥 POPULAR MULTICLASS COMBOS
Paladin 2 / Sorcerer X: "Sorcadin" — Divine Smite + Quickened Spell = massive burst. CHA dipakai keduanya. Fenomenal di tier 2-3.
Rogue 3 / Ranger X: Gloom Stalker + Assassin = insane round 1 nova. Dread Ambusher + Assassinate = guaranteed triple crit potential.
Warlock 2 / Sorcerer X: "Coffeelock" (optional rest rules) — Warlock untuk slot + Sorcery Points engine. Paling kuat di level menengah.
Fighter 1-2 / Wizard X: Con save proficiency + action surge = cast 2 spells in 1 turn at Lv 7+. Shield reaction. Durability boost.
Barbarian / Rogue: Reckless Attack = auto Advantage = Sneak Attack every hit. Brutal Sneak hybrid. Good survivability.
Monk / Ranger: Mobile + Hunter's Mark for highly mobile skirmisher. Dexterous melee with bonus damage.
Selalu diskusikan combo dengan DM. Beberapa combo (Coffeelock) mungkin dibatasi untuk keseimbangan game.
⚠ TIPS & PERHATIAN MULTICLASS
✅ Splash 1-2 level (ambil sedikit level di class lain) sering lebih efisien daripada split rata.
✅ Cek subclass timing — banyak subclass kuat baru unlocked di level 3. Jangan masuk class baru sebelum dapat subclass class utama.
✅ Extra Attack tidak stack — Fighter 5 / Barbarian 5 tetap hanya punya Extra Attack 1× (tidak jadi 2×).
✅ ASI lebih sedikit — Multiclass berarti kamu mencapai level 4, 8, 12 per-class lebih lambat → ability score growth lebih lambat.
❌ Jangan multiclass terlalu dini — kehilangan class capstone (Lv 20 feature) dan delayed spell progression. Biasanya optimal 1-3 level splash.
❌ MAD (Multiple Attribute Dependent) — pastikan class kombinasi tidak butuh terlalu banyak ability score berbeda (Monk/Paladin butuh STR/DEX/WIS/CHA = susah).

Verbal spell components tidak terpengaruh.

Tidak bisa menyerang atau menargetkan charmer dengan serangan berbahaya.
Charmer punya Advantage di semua social ability checks terhadap creature ini.

Disadvantage di ability checks dan attack rolls selama sumber ketakutan terlihat.
Tidak bisa bergerak mendekati sumber ketakutan.
⬇ PRONE
Attacks oleh prone creature: Disadvantage.
Attacks terhadap prone: Melee Advantage, Ranged Disadvantage.
Bangun = gunakan setengah speed. Merangkak = speed ½.
🔗 RESTRAINED
Speed jadi 0. Attack rolls: Disadvantage.
Attacks terhadap restrained: Advantage.
DEX saving throws: Disadvantage.
🧊 GRAPPLED
Speed jadi 0, tidak bisa mendapat speed bonus apapun.
Berakhir jika grappler di-incapacitate, atau creature dibawa keluar reach.
Bisa dilepas: STR (Athletics) vs STR (Athletics) atau DEX (Acrobatics).
🔒 INCAPACITATED
Tidak bisa mengambil Actions atau Reactions.
Banyak kondisi lain (Stunned, Paralyzed, dll) juga menyebabkan Incapacitated.
🧊 PARALYZED
Incapacitated + Speed 0.
Auto-fail STR & DEX saving throws.
Attacks terhadap paralyzed: Advantage. Hit dalam 5 ft = Critical Hit otomatis.
👁 INVISIBLE
Attacks oleh invisible creature: Advantage.
Attacks terhadap invisible: Disadvantage.
Lokasi masih bisa dideteksi lewat suara, jejak, dll.
😵 STUNNED
Incapacitated. Tidak bisa bergerak. Hanya bisa bicara terbata-bata.
Auto-fail STR & DEX saves.
Attacks terhadap stunned: Advantage.
🌀 CONCENTRATION
Ambil damage → CON save DC = max(10, ½ dmg diterima). Gagal = konsentrasi putus.
Tidak bisa maintain lebih dari 1 concentration spell sekaligus.
Incapacitated atau mati = konsentrasi putus otomatis.
💀 UNCONSCIOUS
Incapacitated, tidak bisa bergerak atau bicara.
Auto-fail STR & DEX saves. Attacks: Advantage. Hit melee = Critical.
Creature jatuh prone saat jadi unconscious.

💔 DEATH & DYING
0 HP: Jadi unconscious, mulai Death Saving Throws.
Death Save: d20. 10+ = Success, 9− = Failure. 3 Success = Stabilize. 3 Fail = Mati.
Nat 20: Regain 1 HP, sadar kembali. Nat 1: Tambah 2 Failures.
Damage saat 0 HP: Tambah 1 Death Failure. Massive damage = langsung mati.
Stabilize = 0 HP tapi tidak lagi dying. Masih unconscious sampai Long Rest atau 1d4 jam.
💡 TIPS UMUM PLAYER BARU
✅ Tanya DM sebelum roll — selalu bilang niat karaktermu dulu, DM tentukan DC.
✅ Track resources — Spell slots, Ki Points, Rage charges, Action Surge. Jangan lupa di Short Rest.
✅ Persiapkan spells sebelum adventuring — Cleric/Druid/Paladin/Wizard harus prepare setelah Long Rest.
✅ Koordinasi dengan party — Jangan semua cast concentration spell. Bagi peran (tank, healer, DPS, support).
✅ Gunakan terrain dan environment — Push enemies off ledges, hide behind pillars, set traps.
✅ RP sesuai alignment/bonds — Karakter yang konsisten lebih fun dan memorable.
⚔ TIPS COMBAT
Konsentrasi: Jaga satu concentration spell kuat (Hold Person, Hypnotic Pattern, Spirit Guardians). Jangan buang untuk spell lemah.
Readied Action: Bagus untuk ambush setup — declare trigger, pasang spell/attack, tunggu giliran.
Flanking (optional rule): 2 allies di sisi berlawanan target = Advantage (cek apakah DM pakai ini).
Prone enemy: Melee Adv, Ranged DisAdv. Jatuhkan musuh besar lalu keroyok.
Healing in combat: Healing Word (Bonus Action, range) > Cure Wounds (Action, touch). Prioritaskan Healing Word.
Action vs Bonus Action: Hemat Bonus Action untuk hal spesifik (offhand, Cunning Action, spells). Jangan buang.
✨ TIPS SPELLCASTING
Upcast wisely: Fireball di slot 4 = 9d6, di slot 3 = 8d6. Tapi slot 4 lebih baik untuk Banishment/Polymorph.
Save vs Attack spells: Save spells (Fireball, Hold Person) bypass AC. Attack spells bypass saves. Pilih berdasarkan musuh.
Concentration juggling: Hanya 1 concentration spell aktif. Drop yang lama kalau yang baru lebih penting.
Ritual spells: Cast tanpa slot (10 menit extra). Detect Magic, Identify, Find Familiar — selalu cast ritual kalau ada waktu.
Cantrips scale: Toll the Dead, Eldritch Blast, Fire Bolt scale dengan level karakter. Efficient untuk slot conservation.
🎭 TIPS ROLEPLAY & EXPLORATION
Investigation vs Perception: Perception = passive spotting. Investigation = active searching. Bedakan konteksnya.
Insight check: Tanya DM sebelum roll — "Apakah aku curiga dia berbohong?" Roll Insight untuk baca orang.
Social encounters: Deception, Persuasion, Intimidation — sesuaikan dengan karakter. High CHA bukan berarti harus Persuasion terus.
Rest management: Short Rest setelah 1-2 encounter. Long Rest setelah clearing dungeon. Jangan rest terlalu sering (DM bisa encounter random).
Note-taking: Catat NPC penting, lokasi, clues. Sangat membantu campaign panjang.

⚠ COMMON MISTAKES
❌ Lupa Bonus Action — banyak player lupa Second Wind, offhand attack, atau Cunning Action.
❌ Lupa Reaction — Opportunity Attack, Shield spell, Counterspell, Uncanny Dodge. Reset di awal giliran.
❌ Cast concentration lalu kena hit — selalu pertimbangkan apakah aman maintain concentration sebelum cast.
❌ Spend semua slot di satu encounter — manage resource, mungkin masih ada 3-4 encounter sebelum long rest.
❌ Lupa passive Perception — DM sering check ini untuk trap/ambush. Pastikan nomor updated.
❌ Tidak baca spell description — banyak spell punya interaksi spesial yang jarang dipakai. Baca penuh sebelum session.
